How to Reach?

By Road

NH60 which is interlink connecting Mumbai-Nashik-Agra. The state highways SH30 co-joins from Niphad to Pimpalgaon (B). Also you can reach by NH360 from Sapuatra (Hill Station) to Pimpalgaon (B).

By Flight

The nearest airport is in Nashik (Ozar), at a distance of 15 kilometers from Pimpalgaon (B). International travelers will first have to get off at either Mumbai, Pune, Ahmedabad international airports and then catch a connecting flight to Ozar.Address

By Train

Pimpalgaon (B) does not have a Railway Station of its own. The closest rail link is in Niphad Railway Station, which is connected to Pimpalgaon (B) by road 15km. The important Railway Station is Nashik Road Railway station 35km from Pimpalgaon (B). All the Express trains has halt at Nashik Road Railway station. Also the nearest important railway junction is Manmad Railway Station is at 60km from the festival location
