Baswant Madhukranti

Baswant Madhukranti 2021

"Let's help second green revolution through sustainable Beekeeping"

Baswant Honeybees Park and Training Center, (Greenzone Agrochem Pvt Ltd) Nashik has organised a State level seminar – MADHUKRANTI 2021 on 'Sustainable Agriculture development through Beekeeping’. It is being organized on 22-23 December 2021, from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. The "Baswant Madhukranti" seminar is being organized every year. This seminar is getting very positive response from Bee keeping fraternity especially from Beekeepers, scientists working in Beekeeping, farmers, student & consumers as well. The discussions on various important topics, guidance from industry experts, questions & answers and Award ceremony are the major highlights of this Madhukranti seminar. Due to COVID -19, last year this seminar was organised online, but this year, The ‘MADHUKRANTI 2021’ seminar will be organized Live! All the COVID -19 protocols will be followed during this event.
Key topics to be discussed in seminar:
Current status and opportunities for bee keeping
Subsidy and financial schemes for bee keeping
Protection of 'Dorsata Bees' (Agya Mohol) and role in crop pollination
Towards village prosperity through honeybee village!
Inspection of different species of bees, Hive/ Colony management and demonstration of honey extraction
Employment generation through bee keeping
Along with these,
The Experience sharing by successful bee keepers in various states of North India and Maharashtra.
Success stories of bee keepers
Will also be shared!
The aim of the seminar is to create a sustainable platform for beekeeping & support Agriculture through Beekeeping.
