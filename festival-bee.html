BASWANT HONEY BEE FESTIVAL



Wellcome to

Baswant Honey Bee Festival

The purpose of Baswant Honey Bee Festival is to highlight the importance of honey bees in the universe and to promote beekeeping in India especially in Maharashtra.In this festival we are organizing exhibitions on bees and bee keeping, exhibitions of various honey products, seminars etc. We invite farmers, students, beekeepers and producers and sellers of honey products to participate in this festival.



Event details


  Date 21st to 26th December, 2021 (Tuesday – Sunday) 09.30am to 05.00pm (Everyday)
  Venue Baswant Honey Bee Park, Pimpalgaon (B), Tal. Niphad, Dist. Nashik, Maharashtra (India).
  Purpose The purpose of Baswant Honey Bee Festival is to highlight the importance of honey bees in the universe and to promote beekeeping in India especially in Maharashtra.
