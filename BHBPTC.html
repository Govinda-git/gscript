Baswant Honey Bee Park & Training Center

Baswant Honeybee Park & Training Center is an innovative concept for promotion of importance of honeybee is established at Baswant Pimpalgaon, Nashik (Maharashtra) in 2019. This garden is unique and first of its kind in India. All the class of the society like school & college students, youths, farmers, women, rural-urban population as well as senior citizens were considered while developing innovative concept of Baswant Garden. Tourists visiting Baswant Garden should get education, awareness, and knowledge and skill development of various technologies as well as should also get enjoyment and

entertainments is the prime motto behind development of this project. Baswant Honeybee Park & Training Centre, miniature of ideal village-Savergaon, Baswant food processing unit, Honey processing unit, chocolate factory, sport zone, Rural-Agriculture zone and Food zone are different zones developed in Baswant Garden for full day entertainment, enjoyment, education and get the full satisfaction to the visitors.

Attractions at Baswant Honeybee Park and Training Canter

  • Introduction and History of honeybee

  • Exhibitions and demonstration about honeybee

  • Honeybee miniature village

  • Honeybee painting zone ( bamboo, stone and paper painting)

  • Food zone- Visava, Madhuban, Baswant cafe and food court

  • Visit to Apiary

  • Honeybee miniature city

  • Library and museum

  • Play area

There is also separate arrangement for practical oriented skill based technical training as per the need and demand of the people. This park is spread over two acre of land where all the aspects of honeybee related to health, food, pollination for enhancing crop yield and employment generation are developed. While taking the round in this park all the concept of sustainable rural developments besides entertainment and satisfaction is taken care off.

The Baswant Honey Bee Park & Training Center has been created in an area of two acres. In this park showcase detailed information about the history of honeybee, lifestyle of honeybees, working methods of honeybees, honey bee products. This information is also given through videos. The bee garden has Honeybee keeping equipment and also provides detailed information about it. There are colonies of honey bees like Sateri, Italian and Trigona, from which information is given through demonstrations. Detailed information about bees can also be found by walking around easily through the selfie point and overall structure in the bee garden. It can be said that, this is the first time in Maharashtra that a bee garden has been set up in this way.
